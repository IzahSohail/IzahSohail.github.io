Our Story

Welcome to the whimsical world of NYU Films, a completely fictional film studio. Our journey began in 2023 when our founder, Ms Chuckles McLaughington, had a brilliant idea. She said, "What if we made movies that were so outlandish, they'd make your wildest dreams seem like tax forms?"

Since then, we've been churning out cinematic masterpieces that defy the laws of logic and reason. We've released a total of 3 entirely fictional, non-existent movies that have won awards all around the globe, even in coutnries that have never heard of us. Our movies are so unbelievable that even the Bermuda Triangle has a hard time swallowing them!

"Terms and Conditions Apply" is a sidesplitting comedy that delves into the delusions of university students who believe that hard work, good grades, and a myriad of extracurricular activities are the surefire path to success and happiness. Brace yourself for a rollercoaster of hilarity as they navigate a world where the fine print of life's terms and conditions turns out to be written in invisible ink.

"Den the Alpaca": In this horror extravaganza, Den the Alpaca emerges as an unexpected villain who terrorizes a sleepy countryside village with his unnerving ability to knit scarves at supernatural speeds. The villagers soon realize that yarn isn't the only thing unraveling.

"KFC is the Answer": A fast-food fantasy extravaganza where a group of foodies discover that the secret to world peace lies in Colonel Sanders' secret blend of herbs and spices. As they embark on a quest to protect the recipe from a villainous salad bar magnate, they learn that fried chicken can truly unite the world, one drumstick at a time.

So, sit back, relax, and enjoy the fantastical journey through the world of NYU Films, where reality takes a rain check, and imagination runs the show. Remember, it's all for fun, and our movies are as real as a unicorn in pyjamas!
