NYUAD FRESHMEN STARTER PACK

NYUAD Freshmen Starter Pack is a comedic short film created to humorously depict the common challenges and struggles faced by every NYUAD student. This delightful production is our latest project for the annual 30-minute film festival, where we explore the hilarious yet relatable experiences of freshmen embarking on their academic journey at NYU Abu Dhabi. Join us for a laughter-filled journey through the unique 'starter pack' that defines the NYUAD experience.
