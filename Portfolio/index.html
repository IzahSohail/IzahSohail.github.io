About Me

Welcome to my creative space! I'm Izah, a passionate artist and web developer with a love for bringing ideas to life through design and code.

My journey involves a blend of artistic expression and technical expertise. From crafting illustrations that tell stories to building engaging websites, I thrive on the intersection of creativity and technology.

Originally from Pakistan, I'm currently a computer science student, combining my academic pursuits with real-world experience. I'm also working as a machine learning research assistant, exploring the endless possibilities that technology offers.

NYU Films

NYU Films is a fictional film studio website created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This was my first website I made for Communications lab fall 2023 semester.

Alternate history Comic

In this comic, we created an alternate history of the famous story of the Trojan Horse. We recreated this story with an alternate path: what if the Greeks had built a wooden worm or a bear instead of a horse? We allow the user to select the type of animal the Greeks build (worm, horse, or bear), which takes them along a different storyline where the Greeks were not let into the gates because of the type of animal they chose.

Behind the scenes

Behind the Scenes," a podcast hosted by the charismatic Collins. This podcast falls within the entertainment genre and features Collins as he invites prominent figures from the entertainment industry to provide listeners with a rare glimpse into the world "behind the scenes." The main idea was to unravel the various experiences that never quite make it to the screen, offering a backstage pass to the industry's captivating narratives.
